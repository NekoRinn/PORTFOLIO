About Me

My name is Lyneth Atizon, but I prefer to be called Rinn. I consider myself a passionate and dedicated individual, especially when it comes to the things I truly care about. Ever since I was young, I dreamed of pursuing a career in the medical field, and that aspiration initially guided my academic path. After graduating from Senior High School, I enrolled at Centro Escolar University (CEU) in Makati and took up a Bachelor of Science in Medical Technology (BSMT), completing one year of the program. However, due to various circumstances, I decided to shift my focus and transferred to STI Pasay-EDSA to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT). Transitioning to the field of IT opened up new perspectives for me, and I’ve since gained valuable skills and knowledge that I believe will be beneficial for my future. Alongside my studies, I also manage a small business called Rin Crochet, where I create and sell handmade crochet items, particularly flowers and keychains. This business not only allows me to express my creativity but also serves as a source of personal fulfillment and entrepreneurial experience.

Rinn Crochet

Rinn Crochet began in 2019, during the height of the pandemic. Initially, it was simply a hobby, something to pass the time and bring a bit of joy and creativity during such an uncertain period. Crochet quickly became a relaxing outlet for me, a way to explore my love for handmade crafts while staying safe at home. I would spend hours experimenting with different patterns and techniques, creating small items like coasters, keychains, and plushies purely for fun. At the time, I had no intention of turning it into anything more than a personal passion project.

Everything changed when my aunt asked me if I could make a crochet bouquet for a special occasion. That simple request opened my eyes to a new possibility: what if I could turn this beloved hobby into a small business? I realized there was potential in creating customized crochet projects that people would actually want to buy—whether for gifts, décor, or personal keepsakes. Encouraged by her support and the excitement of others who saw my work, I started accepting orders and began to grow Rinn Crochet into more than just a pastime. Since then, it has evolved into a meaningful creative venture, blending art, passion, and purpose into every piece I make.
